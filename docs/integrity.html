Verify your download

mkeditor-setup-x64.exe me@rowles.ch (0x134CAC00)

It is important to verify the integrity and authenticity of your downloaded software.

Download sha256sum.txt and sha256sum.txt.asc

Integrity check

To check the integrity of your download, generate its SHA256 sum and compare it with the sum present in sha256sum.txt:


            
sh

        
sha256sum -b mkeditor-setup-x64.exe

To compare it:


            
sh

        
cat sha256sum.txt

Authenticity check

To verify the authenticity of sha256sum.txt, check the signature of sha256sum.txt.asc by following the steps below.

Import the signing key:


            
sh

        
gpg --keyserver keyserver.ubuntu.com --recv-keys 0x134CAC00
gpg --list-key --with-fingerprint 0x134CAC00

Please check the output of the last command, to make sure the fingerprint is:

7E88 5361 680D 6F77 3470 3EBD 4CC2 9628 134C AC00

Verify the authenticity of sha256sum.txt:


            
sh

        
gpg --verify sha256sum.txt.asc sha256sum.txt

Please note that GPG might warn you that the signing key is not trusted by your computer. This is expected and perfectly normal.
